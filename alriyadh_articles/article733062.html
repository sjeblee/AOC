Odd Contradictions

Turkey Abdullah Al Sudairy

    In “Al Aharm Newspaper” edition published on September 22nd, 1986; Mr. Ahmed Bahgat wrote an article tackles the importance of Islamic upbringing and the positive influence of embracing the beginning of youth life then manhood with aware religious sense, and within his article he published an announce made within the rule period of Sultan Soliman Al-Qanoni, the Turkish ruler about the necessity to meet the requirements of Muezzin mentioned in the announcement.

Let’s imagine the vast difference between the concepts and awareness presented before four hundred years and those in modern times where the specialists in religion are far from types of culture and the openness of Islamic concepts on the contrary to other religions.

The Muezzin required to be:

1- fluent in Arabic, Latin, Turkish, and Persian languages.

2- a scholar in Quran, Bible, and Torah.

3- a scientist in Sharia (religious law) and Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence).

4- a scientist in Biology and mathematics in a way enables him to teach it.

“We will notice from the announcement form that the fixed conditions despite it are so difficult to be fulfilled nowadays from our point of view were natural and normal conditions… it was a time Islam reached the summit and there was no separation between religious sciences and live sciences.” the Writer added.

This is a document that shows the great difference between past and present, demonstrates the different view to an Imam or Muezzin shall be know variable cultures, familiar to the contents of other religions, and has efficient information and knowledge.

This was the past… but the present many people were content with belonging to certain Path “Tariqah” does not vary from other religions only but also differs with thousands of Muslims’ scientists.

And almost year ago, I remember that I wrote more than one article tackle my observation to certain mosques speeches that repeat the necessity to commit to Jihad, and that the fact we live within enforces the presence of Jihad efforts to hold back the injustice that Muslims suffers from.

Who is the intended with this content? Which country is intended by this?

We already know the current international circumstances, and that the Arab World that Israel was trying to establish peace with it, became after 1967 war who chases the dream of establishing peace with Israel. The major countries have necessary interests connected to majority of Arab countries, and they are dealing with it with the objectivity of their interests… thus to whom this Jihad is directed? Is there an implication highlight that the intended meaning is the Jihad of Al Qada?

It became a pressing need to start with correcting the current Islamic conditions; as Islam is what turns the fact of the living in comprehensive desert community in Pre-Islam age to the living in enlightenment of cultured state was distinguished with objectivity and rational religious vision towards the human rights, his relation with Allah which was not known by Europe before more than 1000 years.
