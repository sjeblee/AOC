Al-Riyadh's editorial

Eyes that do not sleep in the Kingdom!

Yousef Al Kwileet

    Fighting terrorism, money laundering & drugs are three fronts which imposed on the Kingdom to start a war with. Such fronts live and work in the secret corridors among a massive international network which is very accurate in organization and diffusion.

Few years ago, the Kingdom warned Britain that it is targeted by a terrorist action and it didn’t, probably, consider it serious but discovered later that it is real and dealt with it in a serious way by which it could avoid a dangerous problem. In another incident; the Kingdom discovered the presence of a bomb hidden in a package in a plan heading from Dubai to London and informed the United States about the incident which. Accordingly, could get rid of it. The latest event was the preparation of a bomb of high technology by which it cannot be discovered by security services. This bomb was delivered to a double agent in Yemen that could deceive the terrorists through a common work between security services in Saudi Arabia & the United States.

These incident are being mentioned to explore the ability of the Kingdom and the country with which it is cooperating in regard to reaching the core of terrorists organizations by professional penetrations that represent their success standards, as these countries are within the targeting circle addressed by these groups and who cooperate with them.

Yemen has become a main base for Al-Qaeda due to its present circumstances and successive incidents, in addition to the presence of external forces which support the terrorists and considered Yemen as the "soft abdomen" due to its present condition. These factors led Al-Qaeda to spread and become stronger in Yemen. However, the war with it is continuous due to that the Yemeni authority has been feeling its danger after its occupation of towns and villages and its benefit from the disorder of arms selling and smuggling. These factors led the Yemeni authority to prepare plans for striking its bases.

It had been said for long that the Kingdom is financing and supporting terrorism! If we look at those who are being prosecuted from Al-Qaeda System members, Saudis ad foreigners, we shall realize that the Kingdom is at the top of the targeted countries. Who knows how we lived, in the days of the bombings, a case of emergency in the government departments, entities, factories, oil extraction sites and other places. It was a case similar to what has happened to other countries when they declared the state of full alert. However, the Kingdom's ability in regard to accelerating the supporting of its services by the capabilities and necessary technology and its establishment for Terrorism Fighting Center put it in the position of the powerful state that could control any penetration for its territories.

Al-Qaeda's claim that it is fighting for the sake of Islam is a big lie due to that what was discovered in the documents of its leader Bin Laden included his encouragement for looting of property and usage of drugs money and money laundry which are prohibited in the Islamic legislation as much as it is fought, condemned & considered illegal work which aims are dirty in world's laws. In addition; there is nothing in Islamic orders prohibitions & legislation that allows a Muslim to kill his brother. The organization's loss of its basic leaderships in Iraq, Pakistan & Yemen stopped their ability for substituting such characters. Meanwhile, the increase of awareness and the revealing of Al-Qaeda's targets changed individuals' opinions, made it difficult to deceive them and, accordingly, weakened their ability for performing big works. In all cases, the international cooperation was useful in this regard and is the way that should be walked on.
