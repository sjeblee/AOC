Al-Riyadh’s Editorial

Syria is a site for a new cold war!

Yousef Al Kwileet

    Anan represents an international political competence. It wouldn’t be his fault if he had failed in his endeavors as a General Secretary for the United Nations for solving the cases of Rwanda & Kosovo due to the factors of the international circumstance and special reality of each crisis. Yet; his mission in Syria is the most complicated due to that Security Council Members are divided between a front which is allying with Al-Assad’s regime and supporting him financially and militarily & another front which interests do not rise to the level of the moral and ethical responsibilities towards a nation which is being slaughtered, in spite of our belief that politics is based on the immoral part.

Syria is an open yard for a coming surprises that may not be resolved by international forces conflict but by the Syrian nation itself, and that’s why Al-Assad is delaying any dialogue or stopping the successive strikes and army’s withdrawal as such steps will allow the opposition to reorganize itself and prepare new methods for protesting, creating disorder, civil disobedience & adopting the choice of military resistance in a way that drains the authority by many means. Such possibilities may be supported by an Arab & international cover if the dispute stands for long time.

The Russian have chosen the confrontation for various strategic reasons, as they left the world after the end of the Soviet era with neither wings nor supporters, while the world was looking at Russia as remains of a sick country incapable of competing the West that celebrated the demise of the traditional competitor. The dignity of the Russian rose here as they could regain their glitter by making Syria the direct target that it does not accept to lose for the sake of the West the way it lost Libya, in addition to that Syria’s strategic location which overlooks the Mediterranean opposite to the NATO countries & its joint borders with Turkey and other Arab countries provides it with the chance of endeavoring in the complicated roles. In addition to that, this situation provides the Russians with a sort of fake pride by which they feel that their country has started to regain the capabilities of the Soviet Power and affect their circumference of the countries that abandoned the Soviet circle.

China looked rushing behind Russia but tended, recently, to adopt the method of compromises and soft diplomacy due to its interests with the Arab countries which kept opposing the Syrian regime and because it does not want to lose it massive economic relationship with them, especially its goods which are filling the markets of G. C. C. countries and other ones, in addition to putting into consideration that a new problem may occur in regard to oil energy supplies which represent a weakness point it is suffering from. Apart from all of this; we have not been used to that China is a shadow of Russia or the former Soviet Union against which it stood many times, even in the controversial issues with the Western imperialism. Accordingly; Syria is not the bridge over which China can cross towards achieving its high or most important objectives.

NATO countries are not really caring about what is going on, except for what they consider a danger for them. There is no such danger or fear in the Syrian case, except for the rise of an Islamic authority hostile to Israel which is a mere possibility that cannot rise to the level of truth, as Israel is the most powerful military country in the whole region which capabilities exceed Turkey & Iran together.

It is impossible to solve the Syrian condition through the diplomatic way, due to that adopting the courtesy with the Syrian regime means providing him with doses for strengthening its stubbornness and allowing him to continue striking his nation. The end shall be supporting the opposition (parties) as it is the only choice for breaking his power or dragging him back to the rule of rights provision & adherence to the commitments.
