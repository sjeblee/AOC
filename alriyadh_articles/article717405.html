Some of the season’s evidences

Turki Abdulla Al sadiri

    Half of an Arab intellectual; if just a half wanted, with no study, to concentrate his remarks on the apparent Arab Crises and their transformations, including their diversities, and headed, too, towards issuing a book about each Arab country, won’t find tough to do so, as the evidences of events and crises can fill most of the convictions. Yet; no body turns to convictions, as crises come like seasons, where a new regime destroys an old one, benefiting at the same time from its negative aspects, and when the new regime becomes old, the birth of a new regime starts through the various, available & semi-frequent reasons. Actually; what is objectively stable is that such reasons do not head upwards, but drags everything downwards.

There is no objective presentation. There is no presentation that discusses a public interest issue. Let’s consider Yemen, for example, where it is not important to reach or achieve a new ruling regime through a tribal agreement, as what is supposed to be is that the new regime should be reached through a comprehensive understanding which agrees about abandoning all the tribal influences and starting to create a social presence interface that does not differentiate between a tribe and another. Thus; the new Yemen can enter a new development stage from which it has been deprived for more than a hundred years, while G. C. C. countries presence will be better when they support a society, rather than reconciled tribes.

Let’s consider now the justifications behind changing the ruling regimes in Tunisia, Egypt, Iraq, Lebanon & Sudan. Weren’t the high held banners, for assuring the legitimacy of change, promising justice, scientific enlightenment and strong economy? Or at least food and functional security? How did the results come? Nobody can prove that the present time is better than the far past, due to that what has been happening was not a development. It was rehabilitation for opportunities hunters who came from the starts of a previous regime.

Imagine that the interferences of the Super Powers which are very concerned about societies’ conditions and the effects which represent the extensions of their influence, if any, are not there anymore. Imagine that These Super Powers do not need such old interventions anymore, as there are indirect means that can lead to the achievement of these targets, the targets of tearing, dispersion and inner self defeats, with no available evidences against them. The strong world has become stronger in the methods of influence, while Difference and robbery specialists are still the common faces in the Arab social interfaces.
