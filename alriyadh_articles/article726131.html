Al-Riyadh's Editorial

To where the international situation is heading after the time-limit?!

Yousef Al Kwileet

    We said that Syria is not going to adhere to any commitment due to that the authority's mentality focuses on dodging and searching for any outlet which supports its viewpoint from its perspective. Accordingly; it asked for written commitments from the "Free Army", Saudi Arabia, Qatar & Turkey by which they commit not to support the opposition (party. While what's after the time-limit should be decided by international laws supported by Security Council members or the United Nations.

If the Alawites' Authority claim that certain countries which it specified by name are supporting the opposition, and let us believe this; the opposite condition, then, should be stopping the support of Russia, Iran, Iraq, Hezbollah and China which participation reached the level of sending professional killers and streets wars specialists from which some were arrested and confessed about the duties they were charged to do!!

The matter is not restricted on political disputes and judgments which are accepted and then refused as the humanitarian aspect is mixed with the political one, while those who are welcoming thousands of refugees and are being subjected to military clashes, as what has happened in the Turkish – Syrian borders, represent an indicator about the seriousness of the situation and its explosion possibility. The calculations shall change here and accepting the exploding of some countries' borders by claiming that they support the opposition is not going to be accepted under any justification. The case has been restricted by that an authority is killing its people and such act or behavior is classified among genocide cases which constitute international punishments that had been applied before on authorities that committed the same thing and ended by disappearance.

The international situation / attitude is not serious due to that it is not taking serious decisions. Let's say that it prefers the diplomacy rather than entering into hot confrontations. Yet; it is not unaware in regard to what is going on. It is not accepted to watch the regime exceeding the limits and getting drowned in the blood of the innocent. It may be a time limit but it is going to be a short one, too, due to that the issue is not related to official reactions only when the nations protest and push in a similar image of practices that abandoned the international laws and norms and were, thus, refused by the international public opinion.

It is true that the regime is still feeling powerful but it's argument is weak in front of what is happening, while its calculation that the united States and France are preparing for the elections and their interference in any international issue is delayed until the end of elections and counting of votes is not a rule upon which the policies of a higher administration s are or should be built. The case is connected with Israel, as well, as it is a basic engine in the Syrian event. Israel is still considering that the present Syrian regime is serving it better than any coming authority. Accordingly; Israel is not pushing and issuing declarations that push the super powers towards the intervention.

The game is taking place on several axes. Yet; even if the regime did not fall, it's survival is difficult due to that the total destruction, weak economy structure and overwhelming popular tension will not leave the authority stable no matter how much it is going to increase the grasp of its iron fist, while the alternatives in this case are plenty and available.

The Shah who used to control the third biggest army in the world which was supported by the "Savak" had fallen down and "Bin Ali", whose intelligence force was so powerful that the citizen felt doubtful towards his own brother during his power time, followed him, while the armors by which Hosni Mubarak surrounded himself couldn't help him to survive in spite of the security and military establishments that were supporting him. Al-Assad knows that time has changed and that the image of "Kill before getting killed" kept in his mind represent a language that had disappeared with the fall of the last great dictatorship in the Soviet Union and the remains of its stars in the world. The monopoly of authority and privileges by a sectarian minority which is practicing the maximum limits of violence represent a method which is not harmonious with this time at all.

The honorable men in the Arab nation shall stand with the Syrian nation in all cases. It is a humanitarian issue and a duty imposed by the national principles and norms. It is not possible at all to consider the desire of an authority which has vanished due to its retarded method and behavior, if the Arab dignity is the condition and principle.
